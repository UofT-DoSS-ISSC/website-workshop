3.2 Personal brand - what/why/how

Your personal brand is how you promote yourself.

Remeber! This is not about changing yourself, it is about having a better understanding of yourself so that you can find a fufilling fit.

3.3 Values

Keep in touch with your values! They can change priority as you change and your life changes.

Our focus for today will be on the near future.

3.3.1 How do these help?

  • Finding your purpose: Knowng your values helps you figure out what you want out of your life.
  • Guiding your behaviour: helps you behave in a way that matches who you want to be.
  • Helping you make decisions: When you’re facing a deciions
  • Helping your choose a career
  • Increasing your confidence
  • Values = motivations

3.3.2 Exercise 1: Knowing your values

  • Read the values below, add your own if something is missing

    • Don’t think, just circle, star, underline, mark them some way the ones that matter to you/you feel connected to

  • Select ten (10) that matter the most

    • Can they be grouped, are you starting to see any patterns?

  • Select five (5) out of those 10 and define them o What does that value mean to you? o Practicality can mean different things to different people • Once you have your top 5 and them defined think about a time you have demonstrated that value (doesn’t have to be in a work situation, can be school, personal life etc.) If you can’t think of a time you have demonstrated that value, think about why it resonates with you, is it something you aspire to? • Tips if you’re having trouble prioritizing: o Are there themes, and therefore you can select one of the words that is similar to another? o Do these values make you feel positive? What makes you feel good about selecting them? o Be honest with yourself, are you selecting something because you think you should? o Which values are essential to your life (as you know it?) For example, you may be seeking excitement now, even if you know you may later value practicality, what serves you today? o Compare them; if you can’t choose between 2 compare directly, which makes sense? o Visualize a time you embodied the value, or wished you had – how did that feel?
